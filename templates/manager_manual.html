  • Login

    In order to log in, follow the following steps:

    • Enter the login url on browser
    • Enter email and password
    • Enter matatu service name
    • Click login

  • Add new driver

    In order to add a new driver, follow the following steps

    • click drivers on the tab
    • Click new driver on the sub tab
    • Enter all the information required on the form

  • Delete driver

    In order to delete a driver, click the driver on the list of drivers

  • Add new matatu

    In order to add a new matatu, follow the following steps

    • click matatus on the tab
    • Click new matatu on the sub tab
    • Enter all the information required on the form

  • Delete matatu

    In order to delete a matatu, click the driver on the list of matatu

  • Add new route

    In order to add a new route, follow the following steps

    • Click routes on the main tab
    • Click new route on the sub tab
    • Enter route number
    • Enter route price
    • Enter from location and to location
    • Click add route

  • Delete route

    In order to delete a route, click on the route on the list of routes

  • Add new location

    In order to add a new location, follow the following steps

    • Click locations on the main tab
    • Click new location on the sub tab
    • Enter name of location on the search box
    • Click precise location on the map
    • Move the marker to intended location
    • Click submit
    • Enter correct location and specific location and click correct

  • Delete location

    In order to delete a location, select a location from the list of locations

  • Add new exec

    In order to add a new exec, follow the following steps

    • click execs on the tab
    • Click new exec on the sub tab
    • Enter all the information required on the form

  • Delete exec

    In order to delete an exec, click on the exec on the list of execs

  • Add matatu to queue

    In order to add matatu to a queue, follow the following steps:

    • Click on the matatus tab
    • Click matatu to add to queue
    • Click manage queues subtab
    • Select town to add matatu to queue at
    • Select route to add matatu to queue at

  • Add driver to a matatu

    In order to add driver to matatu, follow the following steps:

    • Click on the matatus tab
    • Click on the matatu to add driver to
    • Click on the drivers subtab
    • Click on the driver to add to the matatu

  • Remove driver from matatu

    In order to remove a driver from a matatu, follow the following steps:

    • Click on the matatus tab
    • Click on the matatu to add driver to
    • Click on the drivers subtab
    • Click on the driver to remove from the matatu

  • Remove matatu from queue

    You can either wait for matatu to be removed from queue automatically or follow the following steps:

    • Click matatus tab
    • Click on manage queues
    • class="collection-item"Click on the queue to remove the matatu from

  • Logout

    In order to log out, click on logout on the tab
